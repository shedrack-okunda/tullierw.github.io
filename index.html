BIG FEELINGS? LET IT R.A.I.N

When we tune into out feelings, we can learn to understand and manage them. When those get too big, think of R.A.I.N

R: Recognize - Before you can deal with an emotion, you have to be able to name if. e.g. Oh, there is anger, there is disappointment.

A: Allow - Once we have named the feeling, we welcome it non-judgmentally and allow ourselves to feel if. Sit with it.

I: Interact with others - Rather than keep it stuffed inside, here we reach out to others for support, and help through the tough ones.

N: New wave - Emotions are like wave; they come and go. Here we recognize that we can ride the wave without being swallowed by it, and a new wave will soon appear.
